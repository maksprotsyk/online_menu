EVENTS

In Gachiland Pub, we work hard to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere within our walls. On the second floor, you can find all kinds of board games, a large part of which were donated to us by our amazing customers.

Every Friday evening, we host a competitive board game there where you can win prizes and have a great time!

This weeks game: CHESS!

In the main room there are plenty of activities to keep one busy. We've got numerous classic pub games, such as darts, as well as a collectors rarity: bar billiards. This classic bar game from the last century is a fun spin on pool and is a common crowd gathering point in out pub.

Every Tuesday evening (20:00 PM) we organize a 16-man bar billiards tournament for the prize of having your championship photo above that table! Entry fee is 10$.
